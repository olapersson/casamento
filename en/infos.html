Arrival

For those already in Gothenburg on the 2nd of August, we're planning on reserving a few table for a get together to have a beer or two.

If you've arrived via another EU country, you aren't allowed to buy alcohol in the tax-free.

Landvetter Airport

O aeroporto Landvetter - the main airport, around 30 minutes from the city center.

Cheapest way to get into town is the airport buses - running in 30 minute intervals. Costs 99 SEK per person. Doesn't accept cash, pay with your card or buy on the site flygbussarna.se for 89 SEK:

A cab from the airport costs around 405 SEK, check the fare beforehand.

Gothenburg City Airport

A smaller airport served mostly by the budget airlines. Buses are available and takes about the same time as from Landvetter.

The bus fare is 79 SEK or 69 SEK if bought beforehand at flygbussarna.se.

A taxi should cost about 355 SEK.

Where to stay

Try picking a hotel in the centre of the city. Gothenburg is relatively large and taxi fares relatively high.

A good places to look are Trip Advisor, which aggregates the prizes from several booking sites.

Avalon Hotel

Relatively expensive design hotel, very central.

A classical hotel, recently renovated.
